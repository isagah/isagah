Biography

I am a PhD candidate at University of Koblenz-Landau in Koblenz, Germany advised by Prof.Dr. Maria A. Wimmer. My PhD research focuses on the design of mobile applications for delivering public services. The study analyzes the existing challenges of providing government services via mobile devices and proposes a comprehensive framework for improving the design of such services while considering the complexity of government information processes. I have experiences in e-government, ICT4D and citizen engagement.
